About Me

I am Komal Goyal (कोमल गोयल), a dedicated research scholar in the Department of Mathematics at the School of Advanced Sciences, VIT-AP University, India. My primary research focus is on Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), with a keen interest in the mathematical modeling of fluid flow problems. I apply both analytical and numerical methods to deal with complex fluid flow research problems. Currently, I am delving into the realms of Artificial Neural Networks(ANN), Physics-Informed Neural Networks(PINN), and optimization techniques to expand my research area. With over 2.8+ years of experience in CFD, I am passionate about expanding my knowledge and skills. I am always open to research collaborations and eager to engage in active learning to further my expertise in this exciting field.
