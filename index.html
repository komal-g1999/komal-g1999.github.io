About Me

This is Dr. Komal Goyal (कोमल गोयल), a dedicated researcher in the field of Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD), with a keen interest in the mathematical modeling of fluid flow problems. I apply both analytical and numerical methods to deal with complex fluid flow research problems.

Currently, I am delving into the realms of Artificial Neural Networks(ANN), Physics-Informed Neural Networks(PINN), and optimization techniques to expand my research area. With over 4+ years of experience in CFD, I am passionate about expanding my knowledge and skills. I am always open to research collaborations and eager to engage in active learning to further my expertise in this exciting field.
